About Us

Welcome to Fun Travel SG! We hope you have a good time here.

Who are we?

We are FunTravelSG, a travel agency based in Singapore
We’re a travel company with 25 years of experience, starting in 1997.
We believe that Singapore is a beautiful place with untapped beauty, and we thus seek to spread the word about Singapore’s beauty and attract them to our garden city.
We’ve been operating 3 outlets in Singapore, 2 outlets in Malaysia and 5 outlets in China, and we seek to expand our reach to the digital market.


What is our mission?

We seek to increase our sales of tickets and tour packages by going online so that anyone can book their flights at any time. We also plan to do this via a website for FunTravelSG to sell the packages and tickets, and allow the comparison of different tour packages and flights to find the best price.
